# Privacy Policy — GioFX ForexWidget

**Last updated:** July 2026

## Summary

GioFX ForexWidget does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data about you.

## What the app does

- Calculates Forex session times, killzones, and DST information entirely on your own computer.
- Downloads a public, free, read-only economic calendar feed (bank holidays and high-impact news events) to display market context. This connection does not send any information about you — it only retrieves publicly available data.
- Stores your preferences (theme, killzone settings, alert configuration) locally on your device, in a folder managed by Windows for this app. This data never leaves your computer and is never transmitted to us or anyone else.
- Does not use analytics, telemetry, tracking, or advertising of any kind.
- Does not require an account, login, or any personal information to use.

## Third-party services

The app connects to a public economic calendar feed to retrieve holiday and news data. No personal or identifying information is sent as part of this connection — it is a simple, anonymous data request, no different from visiting a public webpage.

## Donations

If you choose to support the project financially (via PayPal or cryptocurrency, entirely optional), those transactions are handled directly by PayPal or the relevant blockchain network — we do not receive, store, or process any payment or personal information on our end.

## Changes to this policy

If this policy ever changes, the updated version will be posted at this same location, with the date above reflecting the most recent update.

## Contact

Questions about this policy can be raised via the project's GitHub repository issues page.
